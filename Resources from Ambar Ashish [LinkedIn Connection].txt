1. DS/Algo

Leetcode is the closest to the real time interviews.
1. Generally questions will be from String, Array and Binary Search
2. Solve questions using 2 pointer approach and sliding window approach for String and array to get well-versed with it
3. Be confident about the solutions and their time complexity
4. Then go with LinkedList, Trees, Graphs, Heaps, Greedy, DP, Sorting/Searching questions(do general basic questions to get a hang of it)
